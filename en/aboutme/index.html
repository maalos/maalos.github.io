Who am I?

I am an average Smith from the turn of 06/07, passionate about the blinking cursor in BIOS text mode and everything that gets hot.
I live in Lower Silesia, I love warm, light black tea without sugar, I hate to read and I spit on VA panels.
I have a few cats, including Bigos, an orange fluff, which as soon as I leave the room jumps off its soft hammock-like bed hang on the heater, jumps on my the desk and lies down on the keyboard, at the same time hugging the monitor and typing my bank password on the keyboard.
